What I've Managed

Medical Emergencies

From altitude sickness to acute injuries. I coordinate evacuation, manage communication with hospitals, and stay with clients through treatment. I've handled over 50 medical situations without major incidents.

Weather & Natural Disasters

Unexpected storms, landslides, avalanche risks. I read conditions, pivot routes, and ensure safe evacuation when necessary. I've weathered monsoons, earthquakes, and freak mountain storms.

Security & Political Events

Border closures, civil unrest, safety threats. I monitor situations daily and have protocols to keep groups safe and informed. I've navigated multiple political situations without compromising group safety.

Logistical Failures

Lost luggage, cancelled flights, guide no-shows, accommodation issues. I have backup plans for backup plans. When things fall apart, I make them work.

Communication Crisis

Lost members, equipment failure, connectivity issues. I have satellite devices, local contacts, and real-time response protocols. You're never truly unreachable.

Psychological Support

Fear, anxiety, conflict within groups. I manage group dynamics and provide emotional support during stressful situations. A calm guide is worth more than all the equipment.

My Approach:

  • Prevention Over Reaction. Detailed briefings, equipment checks, health screening, weather monitoring prevent most crises before they start.
  • Transparency & Communication. You know what could go wrong, what I'm monitoring, exactly what I'll do if situations change. No surprises.
  • Local Network. I have relationships with hospitals, government officials, rescue teams, community leaders. When I need help, doors open.
  • 24/7 Availability. On every tour, I'm available around the clock. At 2 AM, you call me directly. Just immediate response.
